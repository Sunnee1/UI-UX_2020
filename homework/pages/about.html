More about us

Founded in 2014, we help busy people tap into their most effective, creative and healthy selves by learning to mindfully flow between the energies of doing and being. A recovering busy addict herself, Ellie is passionate about teaching ambitious people to rush less and prioritize self-care because the more we create space in our lives to slow down and nurture ourselves, the better we perform and feel, the more resounding our self-worth and inner knowing, and the more time we have for the things and people we love.

Mission

Our mission is to help busy people slow down and care for themselves so that they have more time, energy and clarity for what matters.

We help participants build a practice of getting off the hamster wheel of repetitive thinking and doing so they can be more available for what matters and have more time to do what lights them up.
